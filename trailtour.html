ABOUT VIETNAM

Vietnam, officially the Socialist Republic of Vietnam, is a long streched country along the eastern coast of the Indochinese Peninsula. Capital city of Vietnam is Hanoi (Ha Noi), former capital of the Republic of Vietnam and largest city is Ho Chi Minh City (also known as Saigon).
Hoi An is located on the north bank of the Thu Bon River in Viet Nam’s central Quang Nam Province, its Ancient Town is an exceptionally well-preserved example of a South-East Asian trading port dating from the 15th to the 19th century.

 Your ITENARY

Day 1


Hanoi – Halong (1 Night)


‘Mumbai to Mumbai tour’ Guests will meet at Chattrapati Shivaji International Airport, Mumbai as per the scheduled flight departure time and proceed to Hanoi. Welcome to Hanoi - Vietnam’s capital city for over 1000 years. On arrival we proceed to the UNESCO World Heritage site “Halong”. The name Halong is derived from the Sino-Vietnamese word, meaning "descending dragon." Tonight, enjoy the local Vietnamese cuisine for dinner.

Day 2

Halong – Hanoi (1 Night)


Today we enjoy a fantastic cruise sailing in Halong bay and visit Thien Cung Cave and Dau Go Caves. We also pass by Dinh Huong Island and Ga Choi islands. Later we proceed to Hanoi. On arrival, we enjoy free time for shopping. In the evening enjoy Thang Long – A Musical Water Puppet Show

Day 3


Hanoi – Seim Reap (3 Nights)


Today we visit Temple of Literature, which is well known as the first university of Vietnam built in the 11th century One Pillar Pagoda, whose origins dates back to the foundation of the city and Ho Chi Minh Complex, which comprises of the Mausoleum of the nation’s founder Hochiminh. Later we fly to Seim Reap. In the evening enjoy the traditional Apsara Dance Show with dinner.
